About Me

During the day, I work as a website developer. This will surprise many people who I have not spoken with recently, because I did not program in high school. Also, I majored in mathematics and statistics. However, I was originally drawn to computer science because I took college-level computer science classes as part of a plan to become a more hireable mathematician outside of academia. Then I learned some website development during my free time because I have always thought the ability to make websites was cool. Web development has worked well as a career path for me because I love to be creative, and website development allows me to combine creativity with my problem-solving abilities.

Outside of work, I like to travel and try new activities such as rock climbing and dancing.
